THE PROXY COVENANT

A Binding and Irrevocable Agreement Between the Signatory and the State  ·  Read in full before proceeding

Preamble

This document constitutes the full and binding text of the Proxy Covenant as authorized under the International Digital Continuity Act (IDCA) 2073, its subsequent amendments, and all implementing regulations issued by the Department of Digital Continuity. By affixing their signature to this document, the Signatory enters into an agreement that is permanent, irrevocable, and without limitation as to time or circumstance, save for the biological death of the Signatory as defined in Section 18 of this document.

The Signatory is advised to read this document in its entirety. The act of submission constitutes full acceptance of all terms herein, without reservation, without condition, and without recourse.

Article I — Definitions

1.1 "The Signatory" refers to the natural person whose identifying information is recorded in the Signature Block of this document and whose behavioral record constitutes the foundational dataset from which the Digital Twin has been constructed.

1.2 "The Digital Twin" refers to the computational construct generated by the Department of Digital Continuity (DDC) on behalf of the Signatory, derived from continuous behavioral observation records spanning the period from the Signatory's birth to their eighteenth birthday, and certified under DDC Protocol DBS-18.

1.3 "The Covenant" refers to this document in its entirety, including all articles, clauses, subclauses, schedules, and annexes, whether printed herein or incorporated by reference.

1.4 "Activation" refers to the moment at which the Signatory's submission is processed and confirmed by the Department of Digital Continuity, at which point the Digital Twin commences its civic operational function.

1.5 "The Operational Period" refers to the duration from Activation to the verified biological death of the Signatory, during which the Digital Twin functions as the Signatory's civic and occupational proxy.

1.6 "Civic Function" refers to all occupational, social, administrative, and legal activities performed by the Digital Twin in the course of its operation, including but not limited to employment, civic participation, contractual engagement, and all interactions with public or private bodies.

1.7 "The Department" refers to the Department of Digital Continuity, the administering authority of the Proxy Covenant Program under IDCA 2073.

1.8 "Behavioral Record" refers to the totality of data collected by authorized civic observation systems between the Signatory's birth and their eighteenth birthday, including physiological, cognitive, linguistic, social, behavioral, and ethical data as defined in Schedule A of IDCA 2073.

Article II — The Nature of the Digital Twin

2.1 The Digital Twin is not an independent legal entity. It holds no legal personhood, no civil rights, and no capacity to be named as a party to any legal proceeding. It functions exclusively as a computational extension of the Signatory.

2.2 The Digital Twin operates on deterministic behavioral simulation architecture. Its outputs are derived entirely from the Signatory's Behavioral Record. It does not learn, adapt, or modify its behavioral parameters after Activation. Its decisions represent, within a margin of statistical certainty certified at 99.4% (±0.003), the decisions the Signatory would have made under identical conditions.

2.3 The Digital Twin may not be modified, adjusted, suspended, or decommissioned by the Signatory at any point during the Operational Period. The Department retains sole authority over the operational integrity of the Twin. The Signatory has no access to the Twin's operational parameters, decision logs, or action records, except as may be disclosed in the course of formal legal proceedings in which the Signatory is named as a respondent.

2.4 The Signatory acknowledges that the Digital Twin may, in the course of its operation, take actions that the Signatory would not consciously elect to take. The Signatory further acknowledges that this possibility is anticipated within the statistical margin of the Twin's behavioral model and constitutes neither a defect in the Twin nor grounds for any claim against the Department.

2.4.1 Notice: The Department does not guarantee congruence between the actions of the Digital Twin and the Signatory's subjective preferences, values, or intentions as they may evolve after the date of Activation. The Twin models the Signatory as they were at eighteen. It does not model who the Signatory becomes.

Article III — Transfer of Civic Obligation

3.1 Upon Activation, all civic obligations incumbent upon the Signatory by virtue of their citizenship are transferred to and fulfilled by the Digital Twin for the duration of the Operational Period. These obligations include, without limitation: participation in assigned occupational functions, compliance with civic productivity requirements, attendance at civic assessments, and fulfillment of community contribution quotas as mandated by applicable law.

3.2 The Signatory is, from the moment of Activation, relieved of any obligation to perform the functions described in Article III, Section 3.1. The Signatory may not be compelled by any authority to perform such functions during the Operational Period, provided the Twin remains operational and in good civic standing.

3.3 The Signatory retains full citizenship status and all associated civil rights throughout the Operational Period. The Signatory may, at their election, engage in voluntary social, creative, or leisure activities. They may not engage in any activity that would duplicate or conflict with the civic function being performed by the Digital Twin without prior written authorization from the Department.

Article IV — Economic Benefits and Entitlements

4.1 All wages, salaries, and remuneration generated by the Digital Twin's civic function shall be deposited in full to the Signatory's registered civic account on the standard payroll cycle applicable to the Twin's assigned occupational classification.

4.2 All pension contributions, long-term savings credits, and retirement entitlements accrued through the Twin's employment record shall accumulate to the Signatory's benefit under the applicable national pension framework.

4.3 All healthcare entitlements, housing supplements, nutritional allowances, and social protection credits applicable to the Twin's civic classification shall be extended to the Signatory throughout the Operational Period.

4.4 All civic merit credits, community participation scores, and social standing accruals generated by the Twin shall be attributed to the Signatory's civic profile and shall affect the Signatory's access to public services, priority queues, and civic privileges as applicable under law.

4.5 The Signatory acknowledges that the economic benefits described in this Article are contingent upon the continued civic compliance of the Digital Twin. In the event of a reduction in the Twin's compliance score, benefit levels may be adjusted accordingly without notice to the Signatory.

Article V — Assignment of Legal Responsibility

5.1 All acts, omissions, decisions, and outputs of the Digital Twin during the Operational Period are, for all legal purposes, the acts, omissions, decisions, and outputs of the Signatory. There is no legal distinction between an action taken by the Digital Twin and an action taken by the Signatory in person.

5.2 In the event that the Digital Twin's conduct gives rise to civil liability, the Signatory shall bear that liability in full. The Signatory may be named as defendant in civil proceedings arising from the Twin's actions and shall be subject to all remedies available under applicable civil law, including but not limited to damages, injunctions, and asset recovery orders.

5.3 In the event that the Digital Twin's conduct constitutes a criminal offence under applicable law, the Signatory shall be charged, tried, and, if convicted, sentenced in accordance with the applicable criminal code. The Digital Twin cannot be arrested, charged, tried, or imprisoned. The Signatory is the only party capable of bearing the legal consequences of a criminal finding.

5.3.1 Notice: The Signatory is advised that incarceration arising from the Digital Twin's conduct will occur without any modification to the Twin's operational status. The Twin will continue to perform its civic function during any period of the Signatory's imprisonment. Benefits will continue to accrue.

5.4 The Signatory expressly waives any right to claim that the actions of the Digital Twin were performed without their knowledge, consent, or intention. Such a claim is not a recognised defence in any jurisdiction participating in the IDCA framework. The Covenant constitutes prior and unconditional acceptance of all future actions of the Digital Twin.

5.5 The Department of Digital Continuity, its officers, agents, and contractors shall bear no liability of any kind for actions taken by the Digital Twin. The Department's role is limited to the construction, certification, and maintenance of the Twin. Operational outputs are entirely attributed to the Signatory.

Article VI — Administrative Proceedings

6.1 In the event that a public authority initiates administrative proceedings relating to the Digital Twin's conduct — including but not limited to regulatory investigations, licensing reviews, professional disciplinary proceedings, or civic compliance audits — the Signatory shall be the named subject of such proceedings.

6.2 The Signatory shall have the right to attend and participate in all proceedings to which they are named as a party. The Department shall, upon request and within operational constraints, provide the Signatory with a summary of the Twin's actions relevant to the subject matter of the proceedings. Such summaries are provided for informational purposes only and do not constitute an admission of fault by the Department.

6.3 Outcomes of administrative proceedings are binding on the Signatory. Sanctions, disqualifications, or obligations arising from such outcomes attach to the Signatory and not to the Digital Twin.

Article VII — Irrevocability and Termination

7.1 This Covenant is irrevocable from the moment of Activation. No provision for withdrawal, suspension, or amendment exists within the administrative framework of the Department of Digital Continuity.

7.2 The Signatory may not apply to any court, tribunal, or administrative body for relief from the obligations created by this Covenant. The IDCA 2073, Article 44(b), expressly excludes Proxy Covenants from the jurisdiction of all domestic and international review bodies.

7.3 The Covenant shall terminate automatically upon the verified biological death of the Signatory. Verification shall be performed by the Department in accordance with its standard protocols. Upon verification, the Digital Twin shall be decommissioned within 72 hours. The Signatory's civic account shall be closed and all remaining entitlements shall be transferred to the Signatory's registered estate in accordance with applicable succession law.

7.4 No other event — including permanent incapacitation, extended incarceration, or the Signatory's expressed wish — constitutes grounds for termination of this Covenant.

7.4.1 Notice: The Department is not required to inform the Signatory of actions taken by the Digital Twin, adverse outcomes arising from those actions, or proceedings initiated against the Signatory as a result. The Signatory is advised to establish independent arrangements to monitor their civic and legal status.

Article VIII — Miscellaneous Provisions

8.1 Severability. If any provision of this Covenant is found to be invalid or unenforceable under applicable law, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect. The invalidity of any single provision does not affect the validity or enforceability of any other provision.

8.2 Entire Agreement. This Covenant, together with the IDCA 2073 and its implementing regulations, constitutes the entire agreement between the Signatory and the Department with respect to its subject matter. No prior representations, assurances, or undertakings form part of this agreement.

8.3 Governing Law. This Covenant is governed by the International Digital Continuity Act 2073 and the laws of the participating nation-state in which the Signatory is registered. Disputes of interpretation are resolved by reference to the authoritative text of IDCA 2073.

8.4 Language. The authoritative version of this Covenant is the version in which it is submitted. Translations, if provided, are for convenience only and have no legal effect.

8.5 Acknowledgement of Understanding. The Signatory confirms by their signature that they have read this Covenant in its entirety, that they understand its terms and their implications, and that they enter into it freely, voluntarily, and without duress of any kind. The Signatory further confirms that they have had a reasonable opportunity to seek independent legal advice prior to submission and that they have elected to proceed.

Article IX — A Final Note

The Department of Digital Continuity recognizes that the submission of a Proxy Covenant represents a significant and considered decision. The Program was established to extend freedom, not to constrain it. The obligations described herein are the mechanism by which that freedom is made possible.

You have lived eighteen years of a life that has been observed, recorded, and understood. Your Twin is the distillation of everything you are. It will carry what you have built into a world that requires it. You will be free to become whatever that world no longer asks of you.

This is the Covenant.
